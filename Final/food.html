These are what I ate Mostly

Mountain House food

When going on any sort of camping trip Mountain House freeze dried food is a no brainer. The meals are easy to prepare and only require a little water. The meals are heated and eaten out of the bag, leaving you with little to no dirty dishes. I ate Mountain House for the majority of my meals ranging from breakfast to dinner and they kept well energized on my journey.

Kirkland Hockey Pucks

On the meals I felt like treating myself, I just pulled one of these suckers out of the cooler and threw it onto the old coleman grill. After I comboed it with some lettuce, tomato, and pickles it disappeared from my plate.
